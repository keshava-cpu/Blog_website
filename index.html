How to Cook Lemon Rice

Why You'll Love Lemon Rice

People like lemon rice for many reasons. While they have their own, here are my thoughts:

⏱️ It’s a time saver as it’s easy to cook.

🍋 The citrus touch is refreshing.

💊 It’s filled with Vitamin C.

✨ It promotes digestion due to its spices.

🌟 It’s the children’s favorite!

Ingredients

For Rice

  • 4 lemons
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 300 grams rice
  • 600 ml water

Spices

  • 3/5 tbsp jeera
  • 2 tbsp urad dal
  • 1 tbsp mustard seeds
  • 2 tbsp black gram
  • 4 green chilies
  • 1 red chili
  • 2 springs curry leaves
  • 1 spring coriander
  • Ground nuts & cashews

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cook the rice with turmeric powder and water for 3 whistles.
  2. Heat oil in a Kadai and roast the nuts until golden.
  3. Sauté the spice blend with curry leaves and chilies until fragrant.
  4. Mix the rice with lemon juice, salt, and roasted nuts.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve!
